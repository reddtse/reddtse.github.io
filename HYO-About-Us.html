About Us

Hayag Youth Organization Inc. was established on 1985 as an extension to Hayag Family Development Center which worked with ChildFund Philippines. Now, it stands alone as Hayag Youth Organization with members from Ormoc City's poorest localities.

See us in action!

Our Vision

A good and unified Christian youth of the society with perseverance to commit ourselves in doing right deeds and develop our potentials for the betterment of oneself and the community as a whole.

Our Mission

  1. To train and develop members and youth potentials and capabilities to become productive and responsible member of society.
  2. To promote and advocate members’ rights and issues affecting member and to empower them and participate in local governance.
  3. To assist the parent association in the delivery and implementation of programs and services beneficial to the members and community.
  4. To tap and mobilize external and internal resources for the sustainability of projects that the organization may undertake.
  5. To network-coalesce with the international, national, local, GO, NGO. Pos and youth focus agencies for the “well-being” of the youth.
